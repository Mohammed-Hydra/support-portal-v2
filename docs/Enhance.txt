Here are ways to make the portal more attractive:
Visual design
Dark mode – Theme toggle with a polished dark palette
Color accents – Use your brand colors for buttons, links, and status badges
Typography – Clear hierarchy and a modern font (e.g. Inter, DM Sans)
Spacing & layout – More whitespace, consistent padding, and clearer sections
Card shadows & borders – Subtle depth and hover states for cards and buttons
UI polish
Loading states – Skeletons or spinners instead of plain “Loading…”
Empty states – Friendly illustrations or messages when lists are empty
Micro-interactions – Hover effects, smooth transitions, and small animations
Status badges – Color-coded pills (e.g. green Resolved, blue In Progress)
Icons – Small icons for menu items, actions, and statuses
Navigation & layout
Sticky header – Keep main actions visible while scrolling
Breadcrumbs – Show current location (e.g. Tickets > #123)
Quick actions – “New ticket” or “Assign to me” in the header
Collapsible sections – For long forms or detail pages
User experience
Dashboard widgets – Draggable cards or customizable layout
Welcome message – Brief greeting or tips on the dashboard
Progress indicators – Clear steps for ticket status (e.g. New → In Progress → Resolved)
Confirmation feedback – Toasts or success messages for important actions
Quick wins
Dark mode – Theme toggle and CSS variables
Status badges – Color-coded pills for status and priority
