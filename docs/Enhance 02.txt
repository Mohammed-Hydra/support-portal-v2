Here are some enhancements that could make the portal more useful for agents:
Ticket management
Canned responses – Quick replies for common issues
Bulk actions – Change status or assign multiple tickets at once
Ticket templates – Pre-filled forms for common request types
Ticket merging – Combine duplicate tickets
Notifications & alerts
Browser push notifications – New ticket or reply alerts
Sound alerts – Optional sound for new messages
Unread badge – Count of unread tickets in the sidebar
Search & filters
Advanced search – By requester, date range, tags, etc.
Saved filters – “My open tickets”, “Urgent”, etc.
Export to CSV – Export filtered ticket lists
Dashboard
SLA metrics – Response and resolution times
Agent workload – Tickets per agent
Trend charts – Volume over time
Requester experience
Knowledge base / FAQ – Self-service before creating a ticket
Estimated response time – Shown when creating a ticket
UX
Keyboard shortcuts – e.g. N for new ticket, Esc to close
Dark mode for agent portal – Same as requester portal
Custom ticket fields – Extra fields per category
Integrations
Slack/Teams – Notifications for new tickets
Email – Create tickets from incoming emails
